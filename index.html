Technology Society

This is

A school technology association is a type of student association that operates at the college level. It provides a platform for students to develop knowledge, skills, and interest in technology.


Objectives of the Technical Association:

1.Developing technical knowledge – Providing knowledge regarding technical devices, methods, scientific designs, etc.
2.Improving practical skills – Enhancing students' experiences through projects, exhibitions, and experiments.
3.Innovation and ideas – Initiating students for new inventions.
4.Developing organizational skills – Improving leadership and teamwork through organizing discussions, workshops, competitions, and events.

Technical Association Functions:

- Tech Exhibitions
- Project Competitions
- Workshops
- Research Activities
- Proceedings on Information Technology and Electronics
